# Sentiq Jobs To Be Done

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the fundamental needs that users are trying to solve when they use Sentiq.

Features are not the reason people use Sentiq.

They use Sentiq because they want progress.

Every job listed here maps to an existing product flow.

---

# Core Job

## Help me become a better athlete.

When I am training and competing,

I want to understand myself better,

so I can make better decisions, stay consistent and improve over time.

---

# Athlete Jobs

---

## Job 1 — Understand My Own Performance

### Situation

After training or competing, the athlete has an experience but often does not stop to analyze it.

### Current Problem

"Something happened today, but I don't know exactly what."

### Desired Outcome

Help me understand what went well, what was difficult, how I reacted, and what I can improve.

### Sentiq Solution

- Full evaluation wizard: five Likert dimensions + hechoBien/mejorar reflection
- Quick evaluation when time is limited
- Evaluation history browsable by month, event type and emotion

---

## Job 2 — Know What To Improve

### Situation

Athletes often receive general feedback: "Work harder." "Be more focused."

### Current Problem

What specifically should I work on?

### Desired Outcome

Help me identify my next area of development with a concrete, actionable objective.

### Sentiq Solution

- Objectives created during evaluation Step 3
- Coach Sentiq AI objective suggestions (category, priority, behavior-focused)
- Follower-suggested objectives through Red
- Meta Foco as medium-term direction

---

## Job 3 — Build Consistency

### Situation

Athletes know improvement requires consistency, but maintaining it is difficult.

### Current Problem

Motivation changes. Progress feels slow.

### Desired Outcome

Help me reflect regularly after events so development becomes routine.

### Sentiq Solution

- Sports mode notifications (post-training, post-match, off-mode daily)
- Quick evaluation for low-friction check-ins
- IEA Consistency (C) component rewards regular evaluation
- Streak tracking in evaluation completion summary

---

## Job 4 — See My Progress

### Situation

Development happens slowly. Athletes often do not notice improvement.

### Current Problem

"Am I actually getting better?"

### Desired Outcome

Show me evidence that I am evolving.

### Sentiq Solution

- IEA (Índice de Evolución del Atleta) with tier, confidence and trend
- Progress tab: dimension averages, emotional trend, objective completion rate
- Monthly AI report (Coach Sentiq)
- Learning Cards (Pro): weekly and monthly insights

---

## Job 5 — Prepare Mentally For Competition

### Situation

Important matches create pressure. Athletes need clarity before performing.

### Current Problem

Nervous, distracted, or unprepared.

### Desired Outcome

Help me arrive with focus and confidence.

### Sentiq Solution

- 24-hour and 90-minute pre-match preparation banners
- Coach Sentiq weekly preparation message (strength + adjustment + 3 practical bullets)
- Preparation screen with match context

---

## Job 6 — Learn From Mistakes

### Situation

Mistakes are part of sport. The difference is how athletes respond.

### Current Problem

A mistake becomes frustration instead of learning.

### Desired Outcome

Help me transform experiences into learning.

### Sentiq Solution

- Post-event evaluation with reflection fields (hechoBien, mejorar)
- Objectives targeting specific behaviors to practice
- Learning Cards summarizing patterns and insights

---

## Job 7 — Connect With Teammates and Peers

### Situation

Athletes want support and shared development with people they trust.

### Current Problem

Development feels isolated. No way to share or receive encouragement.

### Desired Outcome

Help me follow and support other athletes in their development.

### Sentiq Solution

- Red: follower network with shared evaluations and Learning Cards
- Suggest objectives to followed users
- Public profiles with evaluation history (respecting privacy settings)

# Coach Jobs

---

## Job 8 — Understand Players Better

### Situation

Coaches work with many athletes. Time is limited.

### Current Problem

Cannot understand what happens outside training.

### Desired Outcome

Give me better context about my athletes.

### Sentiq Solution

- Sentiq Org Pulse Dashboard: emotional climate, evaluation volume, attention alerts
- Individual player profiles: evaluation history, reflection, objectives, IEA

---

## Job 9 — Have Better Conversations

### Situation

Great coaching depends on communication.

### Current Problem

Conversations can become generic.

### Desired Outcome

Help me have more meaningful conversations backed by real data.

### Sentiq Solution

- Player development history and trends in Sentiq Org
- IEA and dimension patterns as conversation starters
- Attention radar highlighting athletes who may need support

---

## Job 10 — Follow Athlete Evolution

### Situation

Development is long-term.

### Current Problem

Progress is difficult to remember and measure across a season.

### Desired Outcome

Help me understand player evolution over time.

### Sentiq Solution

- Player profile with club-period and lifetime metrics
- IEA trend and evaluation history
- Organization reports: weekly briefing, adoption ROI

---

# Club Jobs

---

## Job 11 — Build A Development Culture

### Situation

Clubs want to develop people, not only players.

### Current Problem

Development depends heavily on individual coaches.

### Desired Outcome

Create a consistent development methodology across the organization.

### Sentiq Solution

- Organization-wide Pulse Dashboard
- Roster and team structure management
- Staff role assignments
- Organization reports demonstrating adoption and impact

---

## Job 12 — Manage Organization Operations

### Situation

Clubs need to onboard players, manage staff, and track usage.

### Current Problem

No centralized tool for athlete development operations.

### Desired Outcome

Manage players, staff, structure and billing in one place.

### Sentiq Solution

- Player invitations (single, bulk email, CSV)
- Staff and structure management in Sentiq Org
- Settings: organization profile, limits, plan usage
- Billing: invoice history and PDF download

# Emotional Jobs

These are often more important than functional jobs.

## Athlete Emotional Jobs

I want to feel:

- I am improving
- I understand myself
- I have control over my development
- My effort matters

## Coach Emotional Jobs

I want to feel:

- I am helping my athletes grow
- I understand my players
- I am making a difference

## Club Emotional Jobs

I want to feel:

- We are developing athletes correctly
- We have a strong culture
- We are investing in the future

---

# Functional vs Emotional Value

Sentiq provides both.

**Functional value**: evaluations, objectives, IEA, Coach Sentiq outputs, organization insights.

**Emotional value**: confidence, clarity, motivation, sense of progress.

The emotional value creates retention.

---

# Product Implications

Every feature should connect to at least one job.

Before building, ask:

"What job are we helping users complete?"

If there is no clear answer, the feature should be reconsidered.

---

# The Ultimate Job

Help athletes intentionally build the person they want to become.

Sport is the environment.

Growth is the objective.
