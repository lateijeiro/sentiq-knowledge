# Sentiq B2B Strategy

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the B2B strategy for Sentiq.

The B2B model focuses on organizations that want to improve athlete development through a structured methodology supported by technology.

The main B2B customers are:

- Sports clubs.
- Academies.
- Training programs.
- Sports organizations.

---

# B2B Vision

Sentiq helps organizations create environments where athletes can develop beyond physical performance.

The objective is not only to improve players.

The objective is to build a culture of continuous development.

---

# B2B Value Proposition

Sentiq enables organizations to:

- Understand athletes better.
- Support coaches.
- Create development habits.
- Improve athlete-coach communication.
- Build a structured development methodology.

---

# Strategic Principle

Sentiq does not replace coaches.

Sentiq enhances the coach-athlete relationship by providing better information and better context.

The relationship:
Technology + Coach + Athlete = Better Development


---

# B2B Customer

The primary customer is the organization.

Examples:

- Rugby, hockey, football and basketball clubs.
- Sports academies.
- Youth development programs.
- Training institutions.
- Competitive sports organizations.
- Organizations from any other sport.

---

# Customer Problems

Organizations usually face several challenges related to athlete development.

---

# Limited Individual Visibility

Coaches work with multiple athletes and cannot always understand:

- How athletes feel.
- What challenges they are facing.
- What they want to improve.
- How they perceive their own progress.

---

# Inconsistent Development Processes

Athlete development often depends on individual coaches and their personal methodologies.

Sentiq helps organizations create a more consistent approach.

---

# Communication Gaps

Important conversations between athletes and coaches do not always happen.

Sentiq creates structured moments for reflection and conversation.

---

# Lack Of Development Data

Organizations usually have information about:

- Attendance.
- Competition results.
- Physical performance.

But they often lack information about:

- Athlete perception.
- Personal objectives.
- Development evolution.

---

# Sentiq Solution

Sentiq creates a development ecosystem connecting:
Athlete

↓

Coach

↓

Organization

---

# Athlete Experience

Athletes:

- Reflect about their performance.
- Identify improvement opportunities.
- Define objectives.
- Track their evolution.

---

# Coach Experience

Coaches:

- Understand athletes better.
- Prepare better conversations.
- Support individual development.

---

# Organization Experience

Organizations:

- Create a development culture.
- Standardize methodology.
- Understand adoption and progress.

---

# B2B Product

The institutional product is:

## Sentiq Org

Sentiq Org provides organizations with tools to manage and support athlete development.

Core capabilities:

- Organization management.
- Athlete groups.
- Coach access.
- Development monitoring.
- Institutional insights.

---

# Initial Market Strategy

Sentiq Org is designed for organizations in any sport.

Priority outreach markets:

- Rugby.
- Hockey.
- Football.
- Basketball.

These sports provide strong club structures, youth categories and coach-led development environments. They are initial validation priorities, not product boundaries.

---

# Broader Sports Coverage

Sentiq can serve volleyball, tennis, athletics, swimming and other team or individual sports whenever the athlete-development need and organization model are present.

---

# Commercial Philosophy

Sentiq should not be sold as software.

The product should be positioned as:

A methodology for athlete development supported by technology.

---

# Sales Message

Avoid:

"We have an athlete management app."

Prefer:

"We help clubs develop athletes through reflection, objectives and continuous improvement."

# B2B Success Metrics

A successful implementation means:

- Coaches actively use Sentiq.
- Athletes complete evaluations.
- Objectives are created.
- Development conversations improve.
- The organization adopts a development culture.

---

# B2B Retention

Organizations continue using Sentiq when:

- Coaches see value.
- Athletes participate.
- The club integrates it into its methodology.
- Development impact becomes visible.

---

# B2B Growth Strategy

Main channels:

- Founder network.
- Club relationships.
- Coaches.
- Sports communities.
- Partnerships.
- Events.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Sentiq becomes the development platform used by sports organizations to help athletes grow intentionally.

---

# Final Principle

Sentiq B2B is not about selling technology to clubs.

It is about helping clubs create better athletes through a better development process.
