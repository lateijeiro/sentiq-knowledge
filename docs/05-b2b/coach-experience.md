# Sentiq Coach Experience

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the role of coaches within the Sentiq ecosystem and how Sentiq helps coaches improve athlete development processes.

The coach is one of the most important actors for Sentiq adoption because coaches are the bridge between the organization and the athlete.

---

# Coach Philosophy

Sentiq believes that coaches are fundamental in athlete development.

Technology should not replace the coach.

Technology should give coaches better tools to understand, guide and support athletes.

---

# Coach Value Proposition

Sentiq helps coaches:

- Understand athletes beyond physical performance.
- Have better development conversations.
- Identify individual needs.
- Follow athlete objectives.
- Create a more personalized development process.

---

# The Coach Challenge

Coaches are responsible for developing athletes, but they often face limitations.

---

# Limited Time

Coaches work with many athletes.

It is difficult to dedicate enough attention to every individual.

---

# Limited Context

Coaches usually observe:

- Training performance.
- Competition behavior.
- Technical aspects.

However, they may not know:

- How athletes perceive themselves.
- What challenges they are facing.
- What they want to improve.

---

# Different Development Approaches

Each coach may have their own methodology.

This can create differences between categories and teams.

Sentiq helps organizations create a common development language.

---

# Communication Opportunities

Many development conversations happen informally.

Sentiq creates structured moments for:

- Reflection.
- Feedback.
- Goal setting.

---

# Coach Role In Sentiq

The coach is not a data analyst.

The coach is a developer of people.

Sentiq provides context so the coach can make better decisions.

---

# Coach Experience Flow
Coach receives athlete context

↓

Understands development needs

↓

Has better conversations

↓

Provides support

↓

Athlete improves

---

# Coach Features

---

# Athlete Context

The coach can understand:

- Athlete objectives.
- Development focus.
- Evaluation history.
- Progress over time.

---

# Objective Follow-Up

The coach can support athletes by understanding:

- What they want to improve.
- Where they are focusing.
- How progress is evolving.

---

# Development Conversations

Sentiq helps coaches prepare meaningful conversations.

Examples:

- "You have been focusing on confidence. How are you feeling?"
- "Your consistency improved over the last month."
- "What is the next challenge you want to work on?"

---

# Team Development

Future capabilities:

- Team trends.
- Category insights.
- Participation indicators.

The objective is not ranking athletes.

The objective is understanding development.

---

# Coach Adoption Strategy

Coach adoption is critical.

The implementation should focus on:

---

# Simplicity

Sentiq should not add unnecessary administrative work.

The value should be clear from the first interaction.

---

# Integration Into Existing Routines

Sentiq should fit into:

- Training cycles.
- Season planning.
- Development conversations.

---

# Clear Benefits

The coach should quickly understand:

"Sentiq helps me know my athletes better."

---

# Coach Onboarding

Recommended process:

---

# Step 1

## Introduction

Explain:

- Why Sentiq exists.
- How it supports coaches.
- What problem it solves.

---

# Step 2

## Platform Setup

Configure:

- Coach account.
- Categories.
- Athletes.

---

# Step 3

## First Use

Complete initial actions:

- Review athletes.
- Understand evaluations.
- Explore objectives.

---

# Step 4

## Integration

Create habits around:

- Conversations.
- Follow-up.
- Development.

---

# Coach Success Metrics

Measure:

## Adoption

- Active coaches.
- Frequency of use.
- Athletes followed.

---

## Engagement

- Athlete insights reviewed.
- Objectives followed.
- Conversations generated.

---

## Satisfaction

Measure:

- Coach perception.
- Ease of use.
- Development value.

---

# Coach Risks

---

# Fear Of Replacement

Some coaches may perceive technology as a threat.

Sentiq communication must reinforce:

"Sentiq supports coaches."

---

# Additional Workload

If the platform requires too much effort, adoption decreases.

---

# Lack Of Immediate Value

The first experience must demonstrate usefulness.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Sentiq helps create a new generation of coaches:

Coaches who combine experience, human connection and better information.

---

# Final Principle

The best technology does not replace great coaches.

It helps great coaches have greater impact.
